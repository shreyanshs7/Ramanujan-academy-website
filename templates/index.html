Courses Offered



  • Foundation Course

    In RA we believe that a solid foundation is the base for a solid building. A child aiming for IIT’s , NIT’s, Olympiads, NTSE, KVPY or any medical college of national repute, must have a good grip over the basic concepts. So in foundation program we shape the soil for the rigid pot.

  • Super 30

    RA is the first institute in Raipur C.G. to introduce the Super -30 Batch. We feel that student sailing through the IIT-JEE preparation must be focused on the content of syllabus rather than wasting their morning time with those average students who are not focused so a student in S-30 Batch as bound in four days classroom program in the morning of 8 hours session, in the rest 3 days he/she undergoes intense practice session which ensures his/her success in IIT-JEE to greater extent.

  • Medical Wing

    In the session 2016 we are introducing Botany and Zoology classes led by our experienced faculties. After two years of rigorous analysis on drawbacks and shortcomings concerned with teaching methodology in Raipur our experience faculty have developed a compact program to ensure success in medical entrance exams in national scenario.
